# Africanvas
## Overview
Africanvas is a hybrid platform that connects African artists with public spaces such as cafés, hotels, and co-working environments to display and monetize their artwork.

The platform transforms everyday locations into living galleries, enabling artists to gain visibility, connect with buyers, and generate income from their talent.

## Problem

Many African artists face:
- Limited exposure to potential buyers  
- Unstable and inconsistent income  
- High fees and restrictions from online platforms  

At the same time, many public spaces have unused walls that could serve as exhibition areas.

## Solution

Africanvas bridges this gap by creating a system where:

1. Artists submit their artwork  
2. Partner venues display the artwork physically  
3. Customers discover the art in real spaces  
4. Customers connect directly with artists  

This creates a win-win ecosystem:
- Artists gain exposure and income  
- Businesses enhance their space and customer experience  
- Customers discover and support local talent  


## Features (Prototype)

- Artist artwork submission interface  
- Artwork display layout (gallery-style UI)  
- Structured platform flow connecting:
  - Artists
  - Venues
  - Customers

## How It Works

```

Artist → Africanvas Platform → Public Space → Customer
```
- Artists upload their work to the platform
- Africanvas connects with partner venues
- Artwork is displayed physically in those spaces
- Customers discover and interact with the artist

## Business Model

Africanvas is designed to be sustainable through:

Commission on artwork sales
Partnerships with venues
Premium features for artists
